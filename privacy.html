Privacy Policy

Yurist AI — AI-powered legal assistant for Uzbekistan

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Yurist AI ("the App," "we," "us") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our current data practices.

Current status: Yurist AI is currently a static demo application. The app does not yet have a live backend server, does not create or store user accounts, and does not transmit your questions to any third-party AI service. This policy reflects our current practices and will be updated before any new functionality (such as account creation or live AI processing) is released.

1. Information We Collect

In its current version, Yurist AI does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account registration is required or available
  • No personal identifiers (name, email, phone number) are collected
  • No questions or messages you type are sent to any server or third-party service
  • No location data is collected
  • No advertising identifiers are collected

2. Future Functionality

As Yurist AI develops, future versions may introduce the following features. Before any of these are activated, this Privacy Policy will be updated and users will be asked for explicit consent where required:

2.1 AI Processing

When live AI functionality is introduced, user questions may be sent to a third-party AI service provider for the purpose of generating legal information responses. Before this feature is enabled, we will:

  • Clearly disclose which third-party AI provider is used
  • Explain exactly what data is sent (e.g., the text of your question)
  • Request your explicit permission before transmitting any data
  • Update this policy to name the provider and describe their data protection practices

2.2 Account Creation

If account creation is introduced in a future version, users will be able to permanently delete their account and all associated data directly within the app, without needing to contact customer support.

3. Data Storage

Any data collected by future versions of the app will be stored on servers located in Uzbekistan and protected using encryption in transit and at rest. We do not sell personal data to any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

Service Purpose Data Shared
None currently — No third-party services currently receive user data

This table will be updated to list any third-party AI providers, analytics tools, or other services before they are integrated into the app.

5. Children's Privacy

Yurist AI is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Your Rights

Since the app currently collects no personal data, there is no data to access, correct, or delete. Once account functionality is introduced, users will be able to:

  • Access the personal data associated with their account
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Permanently delete their account and associated data directly within the app

7. Changes to This Policy

We will update this Privacy Policy whenever new features that affect data collection are introduced, and we will update the "Last updated" date above accordingly. Material changes will be communicated within the app.
