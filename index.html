Frequently asked questions

Common questions

QWhat does Yurist AI do?
Yurist AI answers legal questions based on Uzbekistan law. It searches through 1,200+ legal documents using RAG technology and gives you clear, plain-language answers backed by real legislation — 24 hours a day.
QIs Yurist AI a replacement for a real lawyer?
No. Yurist AI provides legal information and explanations, not legal advice. For complex matters, we connect you with verified legal specialists. Always consult a licensed attorney for decisions that could affect your rights.
QIs my data private and secure?
Yes. Your data is stored on local servers in Uzbekistan, protected by end-to-end encryption, and never shared with third parties. We operate in full compliance with Uzbekistan law on personal data protection.
QWhich languages does the app support?
Yurist AI supports Uzbek and Russian. You can ask your questions in either language and receive answers in the same language.
QIs there a free plan?
Yes — no credit card required. You can start immediately and get answers to legal questions for free. Premium features such as document generation and contract review are coming soon.
QHow do I report a bug or incorrect answer?
Tap the feedback button inside the app after any answer, or email us at support@yurist.ai. Include your question and the response you received so our team can investigate and improve accuracy.
QWhat features are coming soon?
We are working on document creation (auto-generate contracts and applications in DOCX/PDF format), contract review to detect risky clauses, and voice consultation so you can ask questions and get real-time answers by voice.
